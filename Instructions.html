Instructions

  1. First chop the spam and carrots

  2. Lightly beat the eggs

  3. Add the 1 teaspoon of oil until the wok until hot

  4. Add the Diced Spam and carrots into the wok

  5. Add the rice and egg together and then mix everything

  6. Add the soy sauce and oyster sauce with the pinch of salt

  7. Then Enjoy
