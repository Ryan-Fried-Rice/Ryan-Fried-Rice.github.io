How To Make The Perfect Fried Rice

Tools

  • Gas Stove
  • Rice Cooker
  • Wok
  • Cutting Board
  • Bowl
  • Spatula
  • Knife


Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Cooked Rice (A Day Old)
  • A Half Can Of Spam
  • 2 Eggs
  • A Half Of Carrot
  • 2 Tbsp Of Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp of Oyster Sauce
  • 1 Tsp of Oil
  • A Pinch Of Salt

Additional Toppings

Other toppings you can add to your fried rice could have more vegetables like mushrooms, green beans, corn, green onions, etc. You can also add more different types of meat like chicken, beef, shrimp, pork, or chinese sasuage. MSG is also a key flavor to enhance the taste of the fried rice even better to fit your liking.

Nutritional Info

  • Calories: 238-390 per cup
  • Carbohydrates: A significant portion, with 45g in a 1-cup serving
  • Fat: Ranges from 4.1g to 19g per cup
  • Protein: Varies, with around 5.5g in a 1-cup serving
  • Fiber: A moderate amount, with about 1.5g in a 1-cup serving
  • Sodium: Can be high, with 530mg in a 1-cup serving
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Fried rice can provide some potassium, calcium, iron, and other micronutrients
